The most recent out of the TV shows listed, Arcane is a collaboration between Riot Games and Netflix to bring one of the most popular games in the world to life as an animated show. I can’t claim to be much of a fan of the video game, however this is proof that their art and story team does plenty of heavy lifting. Although new, it has hit #1 on Netflix’s top ten chart in 52 countries.

Arcane is an action-filled political drama placed in a fictional world where there is an ongoing class struggle between the utopian Piltover and the impoverished underground of Zaun. There is a mixture of 3D and 2D animation that blends together into an absolutely gorgeous setting, constantly switching between different aesthetics.

This series releases its seasons in “arcs”, each a series of 3 episodes. The final arc of the first season just recently released, so now is a great time to watch through it uninterrupted.
