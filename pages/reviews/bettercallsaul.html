Better Call Saul

2015

Arcane Watchmen (2019) Better Call Saul
Breaking Bad was a masterpiece, and its newer prequel Better Call Saul I’d argue to be even better. The violence and action is toned down and used more sparingly, creating a slow and steady burn, with moments of intense payoff.

Better Call Saul follows Jimmy McGill, a bombastic public defender and his rise up the ladder of status through charm and coercion. It’s realized he’s not only a criminal lawyer, but a criminal lawyer as he is slowly involved in scenarios far deeper than he wanted as he struggles to maintain his strained relationships with friends and family.

The characters are the main selling point of this drama, each one thoroughly fleshed out. All that happens feels natural, and the long-term results of short-term decisions slowly evolve the characters over time into entirely new people.
